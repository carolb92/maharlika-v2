Mabuhay!

Come dine with us at Maharlika - Jacksonville, Florida's favorite family owned and operated Filipino restaurant. Our kitchen serves up authentic Filipino flavors, just like Lola used to make. Masarap! If you're not too full, grab a Tanduay cocktail or San Miguel from our full bar and head to the dance floor for line dancing or karaoke. Our spacious event hall is available to rent for private parties of all occasions. Bring the whole pamilya!
